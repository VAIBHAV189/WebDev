The Taj Mahal is a tomb built in the 17th century by the Mughal emperor, 
                    Shah Jahan, in memory of his wife, Mumtaz Mahal.
                    Taj Mahal is one of the famous monuments of India 
                    Taj Mahal is a marvelous ivory-white marble mausoleum
                    This monument is located in the southern bank of River Yamuna, 
                    in the city of Agra.
                    Taj Mahal is one of the 7 wonders of the world.
